About Me

A Dubai-based makeup artist who specializes in traditional HD look and bridal makeup. With over 5 years of practice and over a year of apprenticeship, she's able to enhance the beauty that you naturally have. She's also trained in events and portrait photography.

Why hire a professional makeup artist?

Making sure that you are at your best on your special day is crucial. You don't want those last minute, unnecessary hurdle. Makeup artists are trained in different kinds scenarios. They know what to do when you need to be under the scorching heat of the sun, if you'll be needing to facie strong spotlights. They know what to use if your skin is dry or oily or if you didn't get that much needed sleep. They'll know exactly what to do to make sure you are perfectly beautiful on your special day. May it be a birthday, your own wedding, or to just simply dress up for the day.
